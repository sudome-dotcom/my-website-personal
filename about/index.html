About

Educator & Learner

I’m a freelance web developer in Nepal and a computer science teacher, focused on building practical and user-friendly digital solutions. I work with businesses and students to create responsive websites and improve their understanding of technology. I work with clients across Nepal, including Butwal and surrounding areas.

Over the past 3+ years, I’ve worked on responsive websites, marketing campaigns, and teaching computer science to students. My goal is simple — to make technology easier to understand and more useful for the people who use it. Whether I’m developing a website or teaching a classroom, I focus on clarity, simplicity, and results.
