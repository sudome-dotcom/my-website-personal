Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Shishir Bhattarai ("we", "us", or "our"), operating at shishirbhattarai.com.np, collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website.

  • Personal Information: When you fill out the contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message.

  • Cookies and Tracking Data: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies. See Section 4 for details.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data such as financial information, government IDs, or health data.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. We use cookies to remember your preferences, understand how visitors use the website, and serve relevant advertisements through third-party ad networks.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function. They cannot be switched off.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the website (e.g. Google Analytics).
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to show personalised ads based on your browsing history.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies. However, some portions of our website may not function properly without them.
